Privacy Policy - FocusForge

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

What Data We Collect

None.

FocusForge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your device.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage (device storage). This includes:

  • Timer settings (durations, preferences)
  • Focus session history (for stats display)

This data never leaves your device. It is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

FocusForge uses NO third-party services, including:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No data collection services
  • No cloud sync services

Internet Connection

FocusForge does NOT require an internet connection to function. All features work completely offline.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data is retained indefinitely on your device
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can clear data within the app by resetting settings

Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • iOS: Settings → FocusForge → Delete App
  • Android: Settings → Apps → FocusForge → Uninstall

Children's Privacy

FocusForge is suitable for users of all ages. We do not collect data from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how we handle data in future versions, we will:

  1. Update this privacy policy
  2. Notify users in the app
  3. Obtain your consent before collecting any new data

TL;DR

We don't collect your data. Everything stays on your phone. No internet connection required. No tracking. No analytics. Simple as that.
