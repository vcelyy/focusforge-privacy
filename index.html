Privacy Policy - FlowForge

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

What Data We Collect

None.

FlowForge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your device.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage (device storage). This includes:

  • Timer settings (durations, preferences)
  • Focus session history (for stats display)

This data never leaves your device. It is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

FlowForge uses NO third-party services, including:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No data collection services
  • No cloud sync services

Internet Connection

FlowForge does NOT require an internet connection to function. All features work completely offline.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data is retained indefinitely on your device
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can clear data within the app by resetting settings

Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • iOS: Settings → FlowForge → Delete App
  • Android: Settings → Apps → FlowForge → Uninstall

Children's Privacy

FlowForge is suitable for users of all ages. We do not collect data from users of any age.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Access and Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • View: You can view all your data in the Stats screen
  • Export: You can share your progress via the Share feature
  • Delete: You can reset all statistics in Settings
  • Opt-out: You can disable optional features (notifications, analytics) in Settings

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  1. Open FlowForge
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Reset Statistics"
  4. Confirm deletion

OR

Uninstall the App from your device. This will permanently delete all data.

Data Portability

You can export your data using the Share feature:

  1. Open FlowForge
  2. Go to Stats
  3. Tap the Share button
  4. Choose your preferred sharing method

California Residents (CCPA Rights)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal information is collected
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information

For FlowForge:

  • What We Collect: Only data stored locally on your device (sessions, stats, settings)
  • Data Location: On your device only. We do NOT have access to it.
  • Sale of Data: We do NOT sell your data. Never have, never will.

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at: Privacy@flowforge.app

GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

If you are located in the European Union, you have certain data protection rights under the GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Restrict: Request restriction of data processing

For FlowForge:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can access, view, and delete your data directly in the App
  • We do NOT have access to your data
  • We do NOT transfer data outside the EU (data doesn't leave your device)

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at: Privacy@flowforge.app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified via:

  • App Store update description
  • In-app notification (for major changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

TL;DR

We don't collect your data. Everything stays on your phone. No internet connection required. No tracking. No analytics. You have full control over your data including the right to access, delete, and export it. We respect GDPR and CCPA rights. Simple as that.
